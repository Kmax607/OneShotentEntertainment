BODY COUNT

BODY COUNT is One Shot Entertainment’s first feature film.

Written and Directed by Kieran Sherry, Body Count is the ultimate love letter to the horror genre.

Logline: A group of teens, a summer camp, and a killer… hasn’t this been done to death?

Synopsis: Amber Thompson is the quintessential mean girl. Gorgeous, cruel, and popular. But when she’s killed on the first night of camp, her story doesn’t end. She wakes up and finds herself in another horror movie: new world, new style, but the same target on her back...

As she spirals through increasingly unhinged realities, Amber realizes the truth. She’s trapped in a cycle of punishment, forced to die, because the genre has decided who she is. But with each reset, she grows sharper, angrier, and more self-aware, until finally, she's ready to break the rules of horror altogether.

Friendships, sleepovers, high-school plays, short films, everything we’ve experienced has led to this movie. Now nearing the end of college, we believe that producing a feature will further our careers, teach us invaluable lessons, and ultimately serve as a fitting conclusion to our years of schooling.

In the tradition of all the great independent horror movies before us… we have no money. But that won’t stop us! We are currently raising the necessary funds, and searching for cast and crew members who will bring this project to life!
