Our Goals

The idea of “Making it” always seemed like such a distant dream in our eyes. That being said, the film industry is changing in countless ways. Large studios continue to fund massive projects, but sometimes sacrifice creativity and artistic integrity. As a result, there has never been a better time for up and coming indie filmmakers to ease their way into the industry. Embracing practical effects, low-budgets, but most importantly, a heart over cash mindset.

We firmly believe that TOGETHER independent filmmakers can persevere. We’re looking to assemble passionate artists of all ages and backgrounds, who are interested in film. It takes a lot to make a movie, from cinematographers, to actors, to composers, each role is essential. To sum things up, love the art of film? Have an aspect you’re drawn to? Looking for a like-minded community? Give us a shot!
